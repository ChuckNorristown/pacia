P A C I A

Pennsylvania Auto Crime Investigators Association

P A C I A, as it is commonly known, is an organization of professional investigators established in 1986 to foster education and cooperation between the public and private sector in the investigation of auto related crimes including auto theft and insurance fraud.

The membership of the association is comprised of law enforcement and insurance investigators who are actively involved in the investigation of auto theft and related crimes. The association is incorporated as a non-profit organization in Pennsylvania.
