P A C I A

Pennsylvania Auto Crime Investigators Association


P A C I A, as it is commonly known, is an organization of professional investigators established in 1986 to foster education and cooperation between the public and private sector in the investigation of auto related crimes including auto theft and insurance fraud.

The membership of the association is comprised of law enforcement and insurance investigators who are actively involved in the investigation of auto theft and related crimes. The association is incorporated as a non-profit organization in Pennsylvania.

Membership in the organization is made by the recommendation of one of the membership and approval of the board of directors.

PACIA also conducts training seminars in the area of auto theft and vehicle fraud topics for the Delaware Valley law enforcement community. The announcement of these seminars will be posted on this web site.

DUES & ENTRY

All dues and applications received will be for membership of the current year.


Annual Dues:  Insurance & Non LE - $20.00  //  Law Enforcement - $20.00

  • You may pay the association by check, money order or cash. We do not have the ability to accept payment by credit card.

  • Cash payment can be made at the monthly meetings at which time a receipt will be provided to you for reimbursement from your employer.

  • Payment made by check should be made to PACIA. Your check should accompany your renewal application and your check will be your receipt or you may request a receipt to provide to your employer at the PACIA Meeting from the Treasurer.

  • Membership dues for 2015 are being accepted until April 30, 2015. In order to have your name included into the directory of members, which will be posted on this website, you will need to have your dues paid by that date. You may also obtain a written copy of the directory at the monthly meetings. If you wish to obtain a written copy you should contact the Administrative Secretary prior to meeting date.

  • All questions regarding membership applications and qualifications should be directed to the Administrative Secretary. See the Officers and Board of Directors section for contact information.
